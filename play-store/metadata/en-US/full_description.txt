Hush is a privacy-first messenger built on the open Matrix protocol. Your conversations live on your own server (or ours), not in a third-party cloud — so nobody can sell, profile, or harvest them.

WHY HUSH
• Your server, your rules — host Matrix yourself, or sign in with phone number to use ours.
• End-to-end encrypted by default for direct chats, groups and communities.
• No ads. No tracking. No analytics on your messages.
• Built for businesses too — every paying business gets its own private Matrix instance.

FEATURES
• 1-to-1, group and channel chats
• Communities (Spaces) — organize multiple rooms under one umbrella
• Threads, reactions, polls, message search
• Voice & video calls (WebRTC)
• Push notifications via Google FCM
• Adaptive Material You design — your phone's accent colour, your way
• 12 languages: English, Turkish, Spanish, German, French, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic

WHO IT'S FOR
• Anyone who wants a clean, modern messenger without the surveillance trade-off
• Small and large businesses who want a fully-owned, white-labelled Matrix workspace
• Teams who prefer Matrix over Slack/Discord/WhatsApp for compliance reasons

PHONE SIGN-UP
Sign up with your phone number and a 6-digit SMS code. No address book scraping — your contacts are yours.
